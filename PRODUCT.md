# Product

## Register

product

## Users

EDUMEE serves Vietnamese students, early-career learners, mentors, and admins. Learners use the product to explore careers, take assessments, build learning paths, join community discussions, and book mentor sessions. Mentors use it to manage their advisory work, review booking requests, join sessions, and track reputation.

## Product Purpose

EDUMEE helps young people choose and prepare for a career path with practical guidance, assessments, community support, and mentor access. Success means users can move from uncertainty to a clear next action without switching contexts or guessing where role-specific work belongs.

## Brand Personality

Focused, encouraging, and modern. The interface should feel capable and youthful, but still task-first and trustworthy.

## Anti-references

Avoid role-mixed dashboards, decorative-only glass effects, marketing-style hero pages inside the app, and interfaces where mentor work feels bolted onto the learner experience.

## Design Principles

- Keep role workflows distinct: learners discover mentors; mentors manage advisory work.
- Make the next action obvious on every operational screen.
- Preserve the existing EDUMEE dark mobile visual language while reducing role confusion.
- Prefer compact, scannable dashboard sections over promotional layouts.
- Show useful empty and loading states instead of dead ends.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target readable contrast, tappable controls, clear Vietnamese labels, and reduced cognitive load for mobile users. Motion should be brief and state-driven when used.
